# Performance-Report---Power-BI-Project-
Interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard analyzing $45k+ global revenue. Features YTD/PYTD Variance, GP% optimization, and Market Analysis.

Global Performance & Profitability Analysis
🚀 Business Intelligence Portfolio by Abdullah F.
Project Objective: To identify revenue drivers and profitability leaks across US, UK, and Ireland markets using Year-to-Date (YTD) comparative modeling.

The Challenge: Management needed to determine if the 40% Gross Profit margin was sustainable and if the business was over-reliant on high-volume, low-margin accounts.

Data Story & Strategic Insights
This section adds the "In-Depth" layer you requested by connecting the data to business strategy.

🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis
Geographic Variance: Leveraged Waterfall Visualization to isolate January and March as high-growth periods, while identifying Ghana and Spain as critical underperformers in the Bottom 10 YTD.

Account Risk Assessment: Utilized Scatter Plot Analysis to map 40% GP% thresholds. Discovered that the revenue model is built on a High-Volume/Low-Concentration architecture—reducing "Key Man Risk" as no single customer dictates more than 1% of total revenue.

Time Intelligence: Engineered a custom DAX Calendar Dimension to enable seamless YTD vs. PYTD switching, providing a "live" look at market fluctuations.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
Tool: Power BI Desktop

Modeling: Star Schema (1:N Relationships)

Calculations: Advanced DAX (Time Intelligence, DIVIDE for Safe Margins)

ETL: Power Query (Data cleaning, Merging Regional Datasets)

Design: Custom UI Layout with Slicer-based interactivity
